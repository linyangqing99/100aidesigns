# Roadmap: 10 families × 10 designs

The 100 designs are organized as a balanced research matrix. A slot is only marked `live` after the product, detail page, prompt, and reuse notes are complete.

| Range | Family | Example directions |
|---|---|---|
| 001–010 | Immersive & experimental | spotlight reveal, spatial story, scroll cinema, audio canvas, generative field |
| 011–020 | Editorial systems | Swiss grid, art book, newspaper, fashion editorial, brutalist catalog |
| 021–030 | Quiet product | calm SaaS, focused utility, trustworthy fintech, minimal AI tool, soft settings |
| 031–040 | Future interface | spatial console, AI copilot, scientific HUD, agent control room, mission system |
| 041–050 | Brand narrative | founder letter, manifesto, heritage story, launch film page, craft process |
| 051–060 | Data dense | intelligence room, finance terminal, operations dashboard, research map, live monitor |
| 061–070 | Retro digital | pixel desktop, early web, terminal, Y2K portal, handheld UI |
| 071–080 | Natural & tactile | paper archive, botanical library, clay product, linen journal, wood workshop |
| 081–090 | Commercial conversion | proof-first service, luxury product, comparison tool, waitlist, creator course |
| 091–100 | Playful product | tiny planet, sticker tool, toy controls, comic onboarding, micro game |

## Completion contract

- standalone working product;
- responsive and keyboard-accessible core interaction;
- detail page in the catalog;
- structured prompt with an explicit avoid list;
- suitable and unsuitable use cases;
- machine-readable manifest;
- source attribution and reuse boundary.
